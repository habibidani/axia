How our web app becomes your strategy partner

From your first visit to measurable improvement: we map your company, generate high-impact To-Dos, and then evaluate what really moved the needle.

1
First contact

We capture how your company looks today

When you land in our web app, you either paste your company URL or describe your business in your own words. We fetch and parse the public signals around your domain and combine them with your description.

  • Quick URL fetch or short text description
  • We map your products, services and target groups
  • We define an initial, data-backed starting point
2
Next steps

We turn your situation into To-Dos and a timeline

Based on that starting point we generate concrete To-Dos, sort them by impact and effort, and place them on a realistic schedule. You always see what to focus on next and why.

  • AI-generated To-Do list tailored to your company
  • Impact vs. effort based prioritization
  • Time plan that shows what should happen this week, this month and later
3
Evaluation loop

We evaluate outcomes and improve the next cycle

After executing your To-Dos we run an evaluation together. We can plug in different methods (KPIs, surveys, A/B tests, cohort analysis) depending on your business model and data.

  • How well do your To-Dos match your actual goals?
  • How successful was the implementation in the chosen timeframe?
  • Which interventions should we keep, change or drop next?
Steps advance automatically every few seconds.
